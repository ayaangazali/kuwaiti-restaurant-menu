About Us

Diwaniya Grill was started in 2009 by the Al-Rashed family with one simple goal, to share the true flavours of Kuwait with everyone. What began as a small family kitchen has grown into one of the most loved restaurants in Salmiya. Every dish on our menu is cooked the traditional way, just like our grandmothers used to make. We believe good food brings people together, and we welcome you to our table.
